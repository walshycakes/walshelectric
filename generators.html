Automatic Transfer Switches

Although the generator creates the power, the transfer switch is really the core of your generator system. It is an electrical panel that allows you to safely connect a generator to your home's electrical circuits and is permanently installed near your main circuit breaker panel. It constantly monitors incoming utility power and determines when a true power outage is occurring, switching from utility power to emergency generator power and back again when utility power returns. Options include transfer switches that provide power to only essential circuits or every circuit for whole-house coverage, and some allow for prioritization of optional circuits or load shedding, such as heating and cooling.

By manufacturing our own transfer switches, we ensure they integrate seamlessly with our generators for the perfect system. We offer more options than any other manufacturer to ensure you can customize the exact system you need. Whether it's essential circuit coverage, load shedding or complete whole house coverage, there is a Generac transfer switch for your application.

The three types of transfer switch setups are described below.

Essential Circuit Coverage

Cover just the basics.

By sizing your generator system according to your needs instead of your home’s square footage, you won’t overspend or be surprised by inadequate coverage. Many homeowners choose to cover only their essential circuits during a power outage. Pair a smaller generator with Generac's pre-wired switch, GenReady™ load center or RTS transfer switch for a simple, remarkably economical system that will keep your basics powered during an emergency.

Managed Whole House Coverage

More coverage with less generator.

Gain up to whole-house coverage through smart power management. If it is unlikely you will turn on every appliance at the same time (most people don’t), a smaller generator paired with a Generac Smart power management transfer switch provides whole-house coverage through a managed power solution. The process is simple - non-essential circuits are shed when the generator approaches maximum capacity to eliminate overload, then are cycled back on when essential circuits are satisfied. Give yourself a little more than just the basics without a substantial increase in your investment.

Complete Whole House Protection

Cover every circuit all the time.

Generac's full line of backup power solutions includes generators and transfer switches capable of covering every circuit in any size home for complete whole house protection. The Smart Switch and RTS transfer switches offer home owners options for whole-house coverage when paired with the appropriately sized generator. Never wait for an appliance to take its turn being powered. When the power goes out, everything stays on - no matter how many circuits you have.

Car Power Inverters

Power inverters transform a vehicle’s DC electric power to AC power—just like the utility power that supplies your home. Generac power inverters make it easy to power appliances and charge personal electronics when you’re driving, boating, camping—wherever your vehicle takes you.

Generac power inverters are among the first to offer the advanced technology of a USB 2.1A port, the latest standard in USB power. This lets you quickly and conveniently charge your personal electronics—like tablet computers such as the iPad®, cell phones, and MP3 players.
